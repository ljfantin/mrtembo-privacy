Privacy Policy - Mr Tembo

Data we collect

Mr Tembo displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device data such as advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. For more information see Google's privacy policy.

We also use GameAnalytics to collect anonymous gameplay data in order to improve the game experience. For more information see GameAnalytics' privacy policy.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect payment information

Children

This game is intended for general audiences including children. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.
